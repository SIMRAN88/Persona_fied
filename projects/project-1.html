India's Digital Footprints

It’s Google Student Community’s campaign about making India Digitally Alive one city at a time. In support with Indian Government’s Digital India Campaign. it promotes Digital Marketing and Online Business Management Awareness among local business owners so that the small businesses and enterpriseses may benifit from the booming online shopping culture. Further , our members are working to scale India digitally and improve navigation through Google Maps.
